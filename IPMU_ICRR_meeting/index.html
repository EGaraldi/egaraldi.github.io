IPMU + ICRR meeting

A space to discuss high-z galaxies and IGM.

A regular forum for IPMU and ICRR researchers to ask questions, discuss ideas, present papers, etc. The main focus is (z>3) galaxies and the intergalactic medium, but everyone is welcome.
